It is thus the attempts to use philosophical methods to identify the morally correct course of action in various fields of human life. Bioethics – analysis of controversial ethical issues emerging from advances in medicine. Environmental ethics – studies ethical issues concerning the non-human world. It exerts influence on a large range of disciplines including environmental law, environmental sociology, ecotheology, ecological economics, ecology and environmental geography. Medical ethics – studies ethical issues concerning medicine and medical research. Professional ethics – ethics to improve professionalism. Discourse ethics – discovery of ethical principles through the study of language. Normative ethics – study of ethical theories that prescribe how people ought to act.
