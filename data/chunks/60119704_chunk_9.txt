The Galaxy Z Fold 4 was released on 25 August 2022, and the Galaxy Z Flip 4 was released on 26 August 2022. Motorola Mobility launched the Moto Razr 2022 on 11 August 2022. It was only available to the Chinese market but there were speculations that it could become available to other world markets at a later date. In June 2023, Motorola Mobility announced the new Razr (2023) and Razr+ (2023) to the U.S. market. The Huawei Mate XT is the world's first double-folding, or tri-fold foldable smartphone, released in September 2024. The device can be used with a case that has a kickstand, and a foldable keyboard with a built in trackpad to provide a desktop PC-like experience. On 1 December 2025, Samsung announced their Samsung Galaxy Z TriFold, a tri-folding phone that was released on 12 December 2025, and will release to the US market on Q1 2026.
