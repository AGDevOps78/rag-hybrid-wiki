In March 2021, Xiaomi Technology announced the Xiaomi Mi MIX Fold. In August 2021, the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 3 and Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 3 were released. On 15 December 2021, OPPO announced the OPPO Find N. On 11 April 2022, Vivo introduced the Vivo X Fold. On 11 August 2022, Xiaomi released the Xiaomi MIX Fold 2. The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 4 and Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 4 were announced at the August 2022 edition of Galaxy Unpacked. The Galaxy Z Fold 4 was released on 25 August 2022, and the Galaxy Z Flip 4 was released on 26 August 2022. Motorola Mobility launched the Moto Razr 2022 on 11 August 2022.
